How to Play with Your Partner

When It's Your Turn to Draw

  • You'll see a word at the top of the screen
  • Draw that word on the left canvas
  • Your partner will see your drawing in real-time
  • They'll try to guess what you're drawing!
  • You have 60 seconds to complete your drawing

When It's Your Turn to Guess

  • Watch the right canvas for your partner's drawing
  • Type your guess in the input box
  • Submit your guess when you're ready
  • Correct guesses earn you points!
  • First to 10 points wins the game
